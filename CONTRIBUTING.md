# Contributing to consul-mcp-server

Thank you for your interest in contributing! We welcome community contributions.

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork** this repository and clone your fork.
2. **Create a branch** for your change.
3. **Make your changes** and ensure tests pass.
4. **Open a pull request** against the `main` branch with a clear description.

## Reporting Issues

Please [open an issue](https://github.com/hashicorp/consul-mcp-server/issues) for bugs or feature requests.
